# Sales Follow-Up Sequence — Best Practices Research

## Optimal Timing
- **First follow-up**: 24–48 hours after initial contact (when interest is fresh)
- **Second follow-up**: 3–5 days later
- **Third follow-up**: 7–10 days later
- **Subsequent follow-ups**: Space out to every 2–3 weeks
- **Best days**: Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday (avoid Mondays/Fridays)
- **Best hours**: 8–10 AM or 4–6 PM (avoid lunch hours)

## Multi-Channel Approach
1. **Email** — Primary channel, most trackable
2. **LinkedIn** — Warm outreach, connection requests + follow-up messages
3. **Phone/voicemail** — High intent signal, adds human touch
4. **SMS** — Use sparingly, only with prior consent, best for urgent/late-stage

## Personalization Techniques
- Reference specific pain points mentioned in their content/website
- Use their company name, role, or recent news
- Mention mutual connections or shared interests
- Reference a relevant case study of similar company

## Number of Touchpoints
- Average B2B sale requires **8–10 touchpoints** before conversion
- 80% of sales close after 5 follow-ups (but most reps stop at 1–2)
- Keep following up for 6–12 months for cold leads

## Subject Line Strategies
- Use their name or company: "Quick question, [Name]"
- Keep under 40 characters
- Create curiosity or offer value: "Idea for [Company]"
- Avoid: "Following up," "Checking in," "Quick call?"

## Follow-Up Templates

### Short Email Follow-Up
> Subject: [First Name] — quick note
> 
> Hi [Name],
> 
> Just circling back on my note from [date]. Happy to work around your schedule for a quick 15-min call.
> 
> [One-line value prop]
> 
> Best,
> [Your Name]

### LinkedIn Follow-Up
- Connect with personalized note referencing something specific
- After connecting, send brief DM: "Thanks for connecting! I'd love to learn more about [specific topic]. Would you be open to a quick chat?"

### Breakup Email (Final Attempt)
> Subject: One last thing, [Name]
> 
> I don't want to clutter your inbox. If timing isn't right, no worries — but if you ever want to chat about [specific problem], I'm here.
> 
> [Link to relevant content/case study]
> 
> Best,
> [Your Name]

## Key Metrics to Track
- Response rate per touchpoint
- Time to response
- Conversion rate by channel
- Optimal cadence per persona
